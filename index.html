"Another Day"

"Another Day" is an idle game that pushes you to become organized and to explore the different worlds that are within the universe of "Another Day". It counts the days that you have been playing for, and it also has different items you can use to make your chances of getting better items higher. The game was done with the help of Gabsol, Natsol, and Stol, who were my group mates. It was the first game we've ever made.
